### Slide 1 (Title)
"Good morning/afternoon. I'm [Name], and I'm excited to present Nostromo Guardian - a risk scoring protocol that's going to make Qubic the safest DeFi ecosystem in crypto."

### Slide 2 (Problem)
"Let's start with the problem. In 2023 alone, $2.8 billion was lost to crypto rug pulls. 90% of new launches fail. Investors have no reliable way to assess risk, and launchpads are struggling to filter out bad actors. This is a massive problem that's eroding trust in the entire ecosystem."

### Slide 3 (Solution)
"Nostromo Guardian solves this with automated, transparent risk assessment. We analyze every project across 7 critical dimensions, generate a simple 0-100 score, verify it on-chain via Qubic smart contract, and provide an interactive sandbox for founders to optimize before launch."

### Slide 4 (How It Works)
"Here's how it works: A project submits their information. Our algorithm analyzes 7 dimensions - from tokenomics to team history to security audits. We calculate a composite score, assign a color-coded grade, and publish it to the Qubic blockchain for immutable verification."

### Slide 5 (Features)
"We've built four core features: A monitoring dashboard for investors, an interactive sandbox for builders to optimize their launch parameters, on-chain verification for trust, and a public API so any launchpad can integrate our scoring."

### Slide 6 (Users)
"This benefits three key groups: Investors get transparency before they invest. Builders get actionable feedback to improve their projects. And launchpads get systematic filtering to reduce liability and attract quality projects."

### Slide 7 (Tech Stack)
"We're built on production-ready technology: Next.js and TypeScript for the frontend, Node.js and PostgreSQL for the backend, and most importantly - a Qubic smart contract written in C++ for immutable score verification."

### Slide 8 (Why Qubic)
"We chose Qubic for four reasons: Speed - fast finality for real-time updates. Security - robust consensus and immutable storage. Cost - affordable fees for high volume. And ecosystem growth - as Qubic's DeFi ecosystem expands, trust infrastructure becomes essential."

### Slide 9 (Contract)
"Our smart contract is live on Qubic Testnet right now. You can verify it yourself on the explorer. This ensures all scores are immutable and publicly auditable - no one can manipulate the data."

### Slide 10 (Business)

### Slide 11 (Roadmap)

### Timing (4-5 minutes total)
- Slides 1-2: 45 seconds (Problem setup)
- Slides 3-6: 2 minutes (Solution and features)
- Slides 7-9: 1 minute (Technical credibility)
- Slides 10-11: 1 minute (Business viability)
- Slide 12: 15 seconds (CTA)

### Delivery Tips
1. **Start Strong**: Hook them with the $2.8B stat
2. **Show Passion**: You built this in 3 days - be proud!
3. **Be Confident**: You know this better than anyone
4. **Make Eye Contact**: Look at camera, not slides
5. **Vary Your Pace**: Slow down for important points
6. **End Powerfully**: "Let's make Qubic the safest ecosystem"

### Recording Setup
- **Lighting**: Face a window or use a lamp
- **Audio**: Use headset mic for clarity
- **Background**: Clean, professional
- **Camera**: Eye level, centered
- **Screen**: Share slides in presentation mode
