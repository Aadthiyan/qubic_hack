# Nostromo Guardian - Pitch Deck Content

## Slide Design Guidelines

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: #60a5fa (Blue) - Trust, Technology
- Secondary: #a855f7 (Purple) - Innovation
- Accent: #10b981 (Green) - Success, Safety
- Warning: #f59e0b (Amber) - Caution
- Danger: #ef4444 (Red) - Risk
- Background: #0a0a0a to #1a1a2e (Dark gradient)
- Text: #ffffff (White) and #e5e7eb (Light gray)

**Fonts:**
- Headers: Inter Bold or Poppins Bold
- Body: Inter Regular or Roboto
- Code/Data: Fira Code or Courier New

**Design Tips:**
- Use glassmorphism effects (blur, transparency)
- Minimal text per slide (max 3-4 bullet points)
- Large, readable fonts (min 24pt for body, 48pt+ for headers)
- High contrast for readability
- Use icons from Lucide or Heroicons

## Presentation Tips

**Timing:**
- Spend 30-45 seconds per slide
- Total: 4-5 minutes for 10-12 slides
- Leave 1-2 minutes for Q&A

**Delivery:**
- Speak clearly and confidently
- Make eye contact (or look at camera)
- Use hand gestures naturally
- Show enthusiasm!
- Practice 2-3 times before recording

**Technical:**
- Record in 1080p minimum
- Good lighting (face the window or use lamp)
- Clear audio (use headset mic if possible)
- No background noise
